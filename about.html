Our Mission

Don't procrastinate, just Calculate! At Pi-Rates, we promote mathematics at NP3. At our meetings, students have joined practice competitions and practiced math riddles to further sharpen their math skills. This allows us to learn new content from each other and review older content all while enjoying the learning process. The Pi-Rates worksheet series is aimed at taking commonly misunderstood mathematical concepts and creating step-by-step worksheets that both help the learner understand the concept and properly implement it. Along with our worksheet series, we have offered tutoring for those who might need extra support in schoolwork and homework. All in all our members get to participate in a support group dedicated to serving our youth through whatever mathematical problems they might encounter. Our club was active on the NP3 campus from 2021 to 2025 and we have transitioned to developing our website since then.
